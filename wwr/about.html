White Water Rafting Company

Highly experienced and certified Whitewater Rafting Guide with over 10 years of experience leading trips on Class III and IV rapids. Proven ability to ensure the safety and enjoyment of guests while providing an educational and memorable experience. Strong leadership, communication, and problem-solving skills.

History

Whitewater Rafting was founded in 2015 by a group of passionate river enthusiasts who wanted to share their love for the rapids with the world.

What started as a small operation on the Colorado River has since grown into a premier rafting company, offering trips on some of the most scenic and challenging rivers in North America.
